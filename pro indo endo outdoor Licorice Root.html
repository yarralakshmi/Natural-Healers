Young licorice plants require consistent watering during the first year, particularly during dry spells. Licorice can spread widely through underground rhizomes, so it is important to manage its growth to prevent unwanted spread. Licorice roots are typically harvested in the autumn of the third or fourth year of growth when the roots have reached optimal thickness and medicinal quality.
