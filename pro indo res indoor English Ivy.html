English Ivy is generally resilient but may be susceptible to spider mites, aphids, and mealybugs, especially in dry indoor conditions. Outdoors, ivy should be carefully managed to keep it from overtaking trees or structures, as it can become invasive if unchecked. With proper care, English Ivy will reward you with lush greenery year-round.
