🍵 1. Sage & Lemon Balm Memory Tea

Ingredients:

  • ½ tsp dried sage
  • ½ tsp dried lemon balm
  • 1½ cups hot water

Preparation: Steep herbs in hot water for 10 minutes. Strain and sip in the morning or before study/work.

Benefits:

  • Sage may enhance cognitive function and memory
  • Lemon balm calms nerves and supports mental clarity
  • May improve focus and reduce brain fog

🥬 2. Spinach-Walnut Brain Boost Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 tbsp walnuts
  • ½ banana
  • ½ cup almond milk

Preparation: Blend until smooth. Drink 3–4 times a week.

Benefits:

  • Spinach provides folate and antioxidants
  • Walnuts are rich in omega-3s → support brain cell membranes
  • May improve memory and reduce oxidative stress

🌿 3. Gotu Kola & Tulsi Infusion

  • ½ tsp dried gotu kola

  • 1½ cups hot water

Preparation: Steep for 10 minutes. Strain and sip once daily.

Benefits:

  • Gotu kola may enhance mental clar- ity and reduce oxidative stress
  • Tulsi supports neuroprotection and stress resilience
  • May improve circulation to the brain
