✨✨Discover natural remedies to support Kidneys health and hormonal harmony—your body’s balance starts from the inside:✨✨

🍵 1. Corn Silk Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp dried corn silk (from organic corn)
  • 1½ cups hot water

Preparation: Steep corn silk in hot water for 10–15 minutes. Strain and sip once daily.

Benefits:

  • Natural diuretic → helps flush toxins from kidneys
  • May reduce urinary tract inflammation
  • Supports gentle cleansing without overhydration

🥬 2. Cucumber-Mint Detox Juice

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium cucumber
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves
  • ½ cup water
  • Optional: 1 tsp lemon juice

Preparation: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Strain and drink fresh in the morning.

Benefits:

  • Hydrating and cooling → supports kidney filtration
  • Mint soothes urinary tract and reduces bloating
  • May help prevent kidney stone formation

🌿 3. Parsley Seed Infusion

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp parsley seeds
  • 1½ cups hot water

Preparation: Boil parsley seeds in water for 5–10 minutes. Strain and sip once daily for 3–5 days.

Benefits:

  • Stimulates kidney function and urine flow
  • May help reduce water retention
  • Traditionally used for kidney stone prevention

🍠 4. Roasted Beetroot Snack

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium beetroot (peeled and sliced)
  • A pinch of cumin or black pepper
  • Optional: drizzle of olive oil

Preparation: Roast beet slices until tender. Enjoy as a snack or side dish.

Benefits:

  • Rich in betaine → supports kidney detox pathways
  • Improves blood flow and reduces oxidative stress
  • May help lower blood pressure and support filtration

🍋 5. Lemon-Baking Soda Alkalizing Drink

Ingredients:

  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup water

Preparation: Mix lemon juice and baking soda in water. Stir well and drink once weekly.

Benefits:

  • Helps alkalize urine → may reduce kidney stone risk
  • Supports pH balance and gentle detox
  • May relieve mild urinary discomfort
