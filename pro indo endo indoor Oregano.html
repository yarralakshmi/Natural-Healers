Oregano (Origanum vulgare) is a well-known aromatic perennial herb that belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae. Traditional healers have used its leaves and oil for treating respiratory ailments, digestive issues, and infections due to its antibacterial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties. In modern herbal medicine, oregano continues to be prized for its potent antioxidants and immune-boosting qualities, making it both a household spice and a natural remedy.
