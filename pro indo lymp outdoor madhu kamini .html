Madhu Kamini is commonly propagated through seeds, cuttings, or air layering. Semi-hardwood cuttings taken in the monsoon or early spring root effectively when placed in moist, well-drained soil. Madhu Kamini needs regular watering during its growing phase, especially in dry or hot weather. Water the plant deeply but allow the topsoil to dry between waterings to avoid root rot. Fertilize monthly during the growing season with a balanced or bloom-boosting fertilizer for lush growth and abundant flowers.
