ü™¥üåø Introduction to Skullcap (Scutellaria spp.)

Skullcap is a flowering herb belonging to the mint family, scientifically known as Scutellaria. It gets its unique name from the shape of its small, helmet-like blooms. There are two common types used in herbal medicine: American skullcap (Scutellaria lateriflora) and Chinese skullcap (Scutellaria baicalensis). Traditionally used in both Western and Eastern herbal systems, skullcap is well-known for its calming and relaxing properties. It has long been used to relieve anxiety, reduce stress, and promote restful sleep. With its gentle, soothing effects on the nervous system, skullcap continues to be a valued natural remedy for emotional balance and mental wellness.
