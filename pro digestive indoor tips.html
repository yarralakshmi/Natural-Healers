✨✨😄 Natural remedy tips for maintaining mouth health:✨✨

😁 1.**Minty Mouth Rinse

Ingredients:

  • A handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup warm water
  • A pinch of sea salt

Preparation: Boil mint leaves in water, let it cool, strain, and add salt. Use as a natural mouthwash—menthol fights odor, and salt gently cleanses gums.

Benefits:

  • Sooths Inflammation
  • Supports Healing

🌿 2.**Clove & Tulasi Gum Tonic

Ingredients:

  • 4 cloves
  • A few tulasi (holy basil) leaves
  • ½ cup water

Preparation: Boil together for 5 minutes, cool, and swish. Clove’s eugenol reduces inflammation, while tulasi helps fight bacteria.

Bnefits:

  • Strengthens Gums
  • Freshens Breath

🍋 3.**Lemon-Baking Soda Brightener

Ingredients:

  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • A few drops of lemon juice

Preparation: Mix into a paste and gently rub on teeth once a week. It helps lift stains and neutralize acids—but don’t overdo it (too much lemon can be harsh).

Benefits:

  • Surface Whitening
  • Plaque Removal

🧄 4.**Garlic Oil Gum Massage

Ingredients:

  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp coconut oil

Preparation: Let the garlic infuse the oil for 10 minutes. Massage gently onto gums—garlic’s allicin is antibacterial, and coconut oil soothes.

Benefits:

  • Reduces Inflammation
  • Relieves Tooth Pain

🍵 5.**Green Tea Swish

Ingredients:

  • Brewed green tea (unsweetened)

Method: Swish with cooled green tea after meals. It’s rich in catechins that fight bacteria and support gum health.

Benefits:

  • Prevents Cavities
  • Protects Against Oral Cancer
