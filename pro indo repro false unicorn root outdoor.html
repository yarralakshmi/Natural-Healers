The False Unicorn Root has a deep-rooted history in Native American herbal traditions, where it was highly valued for its healing effects on the female reproductive system. It was often prepared as a tea or decoction and given to women for hormonal balance and uterine support.

Today, it is still used in natural health communities, though ethically sourced or organically grown material is encouraged to prevent extinction.
